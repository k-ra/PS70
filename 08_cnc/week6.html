door knocker

At first, I considered textures with low depth, such as the moon's surface (from afar!) or a water or ripple pattern. However, I assumed that if I kept my CNC cut to a small area, I could achieve more depth and complexity with it. Thus I turned to the door knockers.

Inspired by old metalworking and fantastical, frightful creatures, I gathered some inspiration photos: I sketched up two main ideas.
